Golf Channel confirms Big Break x Good Good August rollout

Golf Channel Announced 15 Jul 26 Captured 17 Jul 26 North America Tier 1 — Medium mandate confidence
Event summary

Golf Channel confirmed the August 25 premiere of Big Break x Good Good presented by Golf Galaxy, a 10-episode return of its golf reality competition series. The season is produced in partnership with Good Good Golf and features Good Good members as competitors, reporter and non-playing captain. Episodes will stream on GolfPass the day after linear airing, and the winner earns a place in the PGA Tour's Good Good Championship. The underlying partnership was announced earlier in 2026; this release confirms active production and rollout.

Verified facts

Big Break x Good Good premieres August 25 on Golf Channel.

The season runs for 10 episodes and streams next day on GolfPass.

Good Good Golf members Sean Walsh and Matt Meneghetti compete; Matt Scharff reports; Tom Broders and Brad Dalke serve as non-playing captains.

Golf Galaxy is the presenting sponsor and Trex is an official sponsor.

The winner receives an exemption into the 2026 PGA Tour Good Good Championship.

The Golf Channel release confirms rollout of a partnership announced earlier in 2026.

Interpretation

The deal demonstrates a mature creator-to-legacy-media structure: Good Good contributes talent, community and tournament equity, while Golf Channel supplies the format, linear reach, GolfPass extension and sponsor inventory. It is a production and distribution crossover, not evidence that Golf Channel is broadly open to creator pitches.
